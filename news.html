Active Development Milestone: The Warrior and the Smith Enters Full Production

We're thrilled to announce that The Warrior and the Smith has officially entered full production! Our team of five passionate developers has been hard at work building out the core gameplay systems, including the asymmetric cooperative mechanics that define our debut title. The Warrior's combat system and the Smith's crafting & base-building loops are coming together beautifully. With our target launch on Steam in 2027, every roguelike run is shaping up to deliver fresh challenges and meaningful cooperation. Stay tuned for more details on environments, boss encounters, and the original soundtrack.
