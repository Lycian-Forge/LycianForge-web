The Warrior and the Smith

In Development

The kingdom's last hope lies in the legendary Valorstone. A battle-hardened Warrior and a master Smith embark on a perilous quest through hostile lands, combining their unique skills in perfect cooperation. While the Warrior explores dangerous territories and defends against relentless hordes, the Smith forges powerful weapons and fortifies their base. Every run is a new challenge in this cooperative roguelike adventure.
