Forging fun under a swift sunrise!

Lycian Forge is an indie game studio founded in 2024 by us, three engineer friends united by a passion for crafting exhilarating, fast-paced gaming experiences. Drawing inspiration from the rich history and vibrant landscapes of the Lycia region , we aim to forge games that captivate and thrill players worldwide. With a commitment to innovation and fun, Lycian Forge is dedicated to delivering high-octane adventures that keep players on the edge of their seats.
