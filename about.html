About Lycian Forge

Passionate creators forging the future of indie gaming

Our Story

Born from Passion, Forged in Fire

Lycian Forge was founded in 2024 by three engineer friends who shared a common vision: to create games that challenge players and bring people together. What started as late-night brainstorming sessions evolved into a passionate indie studio dedicated to crafting cooperative experiences that emphasize teamwork and meaningful gameplay.

We believe the best games emerge from genuine passion and creative freedom. As an independent studio, we have the liberty to take risks, experiment with innovative mechanics, and create experiences that prioritize gameplay depth over trends.

Our name, "Lycian Forge," embodies our philosophy: like skilled craftsmen at the anvil, we forge our games through dedication, iteration, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Our debut title, "The Warrior and the Smith," reflects this philosophy—a cooperative roguelike ARPG where two players must work in perfect harmony to overcome impossible odds.
