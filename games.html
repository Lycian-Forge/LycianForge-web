Co-op Roguelike ARPG Steam 2027

About the Game

When the kingdom's survival hangs by a thread, a Warrior and a Smith set out on an impossible quest to find the legendary Valorstone. This cooperative roguelike adventure requires true teamwork‚Äîone player cannot succeed without the other.

The Warrior ventures into treacherous environments, gathering crucial resources, battling through enemy hordes, and defending the base from relentless attacks. Meanwhile, the Smith transforms raw materials into devastating weapons, powerful spells, and robust defensive structures, ensuring both players can withstand the challenges ahead.

Each run presents unique dangers across diverse environments filled with cunning foes. Coordinate your strategies, adapt to ever-changing threats, and push deeper into hostile territory. The fate of the kingdom rests on your cooperation.
